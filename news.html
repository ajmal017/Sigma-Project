Minterscan: how the transparent and open blockchain Explorer Minter started

#news of the day #minterscan

On May 15, the main network of the Minter project was launched. in 2017. Then the development team headed by Evgeny Gordeev the idea of launching a blockchain, where users can create absolutely liquid coins in two clicks.

Meet Minterscan, the Explorer of Minter Blockchain

The Minterscan project was launched on May 28 by two people. Its main task was to is to provide users with up-to-date information on the status of blockchain Minter. The co-founder of the project, Sergei Salii, told us that the idea Minterscan came to him unexpectedly: "I was studying Minter for a while and then, playing with the console and the Explorer, decided I could do it my way. I sat down for the code and did not differ from my laptop for several days. Judging by the reviews users, the first version wasn't a bad one."

After the launch of the first version of the Explorer, Sergey had to choose between the main work and the Minterscan project, as both The main work was well paid, but the drive's long gone. I'm one of those people who can't sit and work like robots eight hours from call to call. If there is no spark in the relationship, it falls apart. After the start of the Minterscan, the spark is back, the time for simultaneous the project development and the main work task was not enough. I had to to choose. I do not regret the choice.

Funding, community and other Minter projects and an example of a second line migration

Support for the Minter team continued beyond the preparation phase Minterscan, but also during the creation of the internal coin of the project - MSCAN.

"For... I am grateful to Evgeny Gordeev and the whole team for their support in creating the coin Minter. I will address to readers: if you plan to start a worthwhile project on
Minter, rest assured you'll be noticed and supported.

The interior ministry needs projects, that are valuable to users. The Minter team has published a short A guide for those who are going to issue their coin, I highly recommend with to get acquainted with it and do everything as it says," recommended Sergei.
